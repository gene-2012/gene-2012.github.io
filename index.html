About Me

Hi, I'm a student at Capital Normal University High School (CNUHS). Currently, I am a researcher at the Beijing Youth AI Academy and Beijing Zhongguancun Academy, where I explore the frontiers of artificial intelligence.

My research is driven by a fascination with how machines think and play. I am particularly focused on Large Language Models (LLMs) and Chain-of-Thought (CoT) reasoning. My ultimate goal is to bridge the gap between advanced cognitive modeling and interactive entertainment by developing next-generation AI in Game systems-creating NPCs and game worlds that are more intelligent, responsive, and immersive.
